Lilla Mei is more than just jewelry; it's a reflection of my passion for design, aesthetics, and creating with intention. Founded by me, Meital, in vibrant Tel Aviv, the name Lilla Mei combines my own name with the inspiring character Little My from the children's show The Moomins, embodying a spirit of understated strength and unique charm.

I specialize in minimalist modern jewelry with a distinct personal touch, crafting delicate yet present pieces from gold, silver, and carefully selected colorful gemstones. My designs are driven by a desire to translate internal visions into tangible form, sometimes sparked by an emotion, other times by the beauty found in everyday shapes and colors.

At Lilla Mei, I value creating from the heart with meticulous attention to detail. I believe in the principles of slow fashion, choosing thoughtful craftsmanship over mass production, and always considering the impact I have on both people and my surroundings.

My aim is to create jewelry that not only enhances your style but also carries a deeper meaning, becoming a cherished part of your story.
